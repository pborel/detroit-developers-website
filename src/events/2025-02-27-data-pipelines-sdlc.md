A dual-topic workshop hosted by RIVET Work. The first segment covered data pipelines — how they function, RIVET's pipeline history, and future directions in SaaS. The second addressed software development lifecycle practices for cross-functional teams, exploring collaboration among developers, product managers, technical program managers, and designers.
